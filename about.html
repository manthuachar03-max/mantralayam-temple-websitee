History of SRS MUTT
The SRS Mutt originated from the 13th-century Dvaita lineage of Sri Madhvacharya and was formally shaped by Sri Vibhudendra Tirtha in the 15th century. It achieved historic prominence in 1671 AD when its 16th pontiff, Sri Raghavendra Swamy, entered live samadhi (Mula Brindavana) at Mantralayam..
